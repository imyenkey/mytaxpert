## 🚀 Common Tasks

### View Blog
1. Open `blog.html`
2. Browse posts in grid
3. Search for topics
4. Click post to read

**Time**: 30 seconds

### Create a Post
1. Open `admin.html`
2. Login: `admin123`
3. Click "New Post"
4. Fill in all fields
5. Click "Publish Post"

**Time**: 5 minutes

### Edit a Post
1. Open `admin.html`
2. Login: `admin123`
3. Click "All Posts"
4. Click "Edit" on post
5. Update and save

**Time**: 3 minutes

### Delete a Post
1. Open `admin.html`
2. Login: `admin123`
3. Click "All Posts"
4. Click "Delete" on post
5. Confirm deletion

**Time**: 1 minute

### Search Posts
1. Open `blog.html`
2. Type in search bar
3. Results filter instantly

**Time**: 10 seconds
